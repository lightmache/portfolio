Research Context: Space Sector-Driven Design Thinking

"The Lunar Surface Innovation Consortium (LSIC)... brings together government, academia, non-profit institutions, and the private sector to identify technological capabilities and hurdles that must be retired to achieve a sustained presence on the surface of the Moon."

Through supporting facilitation of a design-thinking workshops for the NASA-funded LSIC, I observed how these cross-sector teams achieve "synchronous synthesis." I adopted their space sector-driven methodology to build internal tooling that orchestrates multi-stakeholder workflows into a single, audit-ready decision stream, ensuring consensus is never slowed by fragmented, asynchronous friction.
